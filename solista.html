SOLISTA

DIMENSIONS:

750h x 410w x 564d

DESCRIPTION:

An ideal small to medium capacity bean to cup machine that uses freeze dried milk. THE SOLISTA can be customised to offer wide choice of drinks selections making it suitable for most applications where a freeze dried milk machine is required.

FEATURES:

  • Produces espresso with a perfect cream and freshly brewed coffee
  • 10 direct selection push buttons
  • 128*64 pixel graphic display
  • Ideal for large office ow workplace
  • Sugar Doser, Cup Dropper, Base and coin mechanism optional
  • Milk Granule 0.7kg
  • Chocolate capacity 1.2kg

SELECTIONS

  • Short Black, Long Black, Flat white, Cappuccino, Caffe Latte, Moccacino, Hot chocolate
