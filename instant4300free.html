INSTANT 4300

DIMENSIONS:

600 (H) *320 (W)* 477(D)

DESCRIPTION:

Ideal machine for workplace with a tight budget but still wants to offer instant Coffee/tea and hot Chocolate options for their staff. Suitable for about 150 cups per day. Due to small canister size, it may need refilling 2- 3 times per day for the maximum output. Can also be converted to four tea options machine if required. Available for free monthly rental. (Conditions applied) OR a very nominal weekly rental

FEATURES:

  • 4 canister & 3 mixer configuration
  • Light weight only 22 kgs
  • Can have its own water tank or plumbed
  • Easy to clean and service
  • Due to its small size can be placed anywhere

SELECTIONS:

Option 1 - Cappuccino, Long Black, Espresso, Flat white, Café latte, Hot Chocolate, Mochaccino, Tea (can have one of these flavors: plain tea, ginger tea with milk, lemon tea, lemon black pepper tea, chai Latte)

Option 2 - Plain tea, Ginger tea with milk, Lemon tea, Lemon black pepper tea
