JURA S8 CHROME

DIMENSIONS:

384h x 280w x 439d

DESCRIPTION:

Enjoy your morning with an aromatic cup of coffee courtesy of the Jura S8 Auto Fresh Milk Coffee Machine. Easy to use, it comes with a high-resolution touch screen interface that lets you select and create your favourite coffees with a simple touch or swipe. Suitable for a small Office can do upto 30 cups a day.

FEATURES:

  • Utilizes the Pulse Extraction Process that accurately pulses water
  • Integrated rinsing, cleaning and descaling programme with CLARIS Smart filter
  • 4.3-inch high-resolution colour touch screen display
  • Fine Foam technology
  • 15 individually programmable specialities
  • Chrome finish
  • Weight: 10kg
  • 280g bean capacity per container, water tank capacity: 1.9 litres
  • Built-in coffee grinder
  • Energy saving mode
  • Hand-polished die-cast zinc, giving it added durability and style.
  • 10 coffee-strength levels and 3 temperature levels

SELECTIONS

Fully automatic one-button brewing of 15 drinks: flat white, caffè latte, latte macchiato, espresso macchiato, cappuccino, 1 or 2 coffees, 1 or 2 espressos, 1 or 2 ristrettos, milk foam, hot milk, hot water and hot water for green tea.

SUITABLE FOR:

  • Workplace
  • Workshop
  • Office
  • Hotels
  • Restaurants
  • Hairdressers
  • Dairy and Takeaways
