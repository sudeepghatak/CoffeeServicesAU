    JL 32A FRESH MILK

    DIMENSIONS:

    620h*350w*560d

    DESCRIPTION:

    • Built with a 10.1 inch touch screen and designed with an intuitive menu, it caters to a diversity of quality coffee drinks with excellent efficiency.
    • It's dual systems ensure both consistency and speed, delivering high-quality beverages promptly. With a versatile range of flavors and customizable options, the JL32A caters to diverse preferences, making it the ideal choice for those who appreciate excellence in every cup.

    FEATURES:

    • Dual Powder Canisters
      • JL32A boasts a substantial 1.5kg bean hopper and dual powder compartments, enabling it to offer a wide range of beverage flavors. This versatility ensures that every customer finds a drink that suits their taste, making it ideal for business settings looking to satisfy varied consumer demands efficiently. Suitable to dispense 200 Cups a day.
    • Dual Pumps and Dual Boilers
      • JL32A is engineered with dual pumps and boilers, allowing simultaneous coffee extraction and milk frothing. This feature ensures efficient and professional beverage preparation, with coffee and milk being dispensed at the same time. It's perfect for commercial settings, significantly enhancing productivity and meeting the high demands of a professional environment.
    • Multiple Water Supply
      • JL32A offers versatile water supply options, including a built-in 4L water tank, compatibility with bottled water, and direct connection to a mains water supply. This flexibility makes it exceptionally convenient and adaptable for various commercial environments.
