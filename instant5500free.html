INSTANT 5500

DIMENSIONS:

785 (H) *350 (W)* 500(D)

DESCRIPTION:

This machine is ideal for busy & large factories and workplaces. Suitable for 150 – 200 cups per day. Can do multiple instant Coffee and tea selections and has a proven track record. The machine can also be programmed to dispense real strong instant coffee. Available on a free monthly rental (Condition: Minimum Ingredient quantity to be purchased) OR on a nominal weekly rental.

FEATURES:

  • Has enough space to place a big cup or a jug
  • 5 canister & 5 mixer configuration
  • Light weight only 28 kgs
  • Can have its own water tank or plumbed
  • Easy to clean and service
  • Wide range of coffee & tea selections

SELECTIONS:

Cappuccino, Long Black, Espresso, Chocolate ,Mochaccino, Chai latte Milk, Hot Water, Flat White, Plain tea, Lemon tea, Ginger Tea with milk, Coffee with Milk (Can have 11 possible selections including 2 tea selections)
