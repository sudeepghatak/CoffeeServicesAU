Our Company History

OUR TRUE LOVE

We have to admit that although we got lots of interests, we have really got one true passion: A dark, hot, drink called… COFFEE and another dark golden drink TEA

We love coffee & tea, and we love making it: espresso… mocha… cappuccino… ginger… masala… cardamom chai even cold with ice.

No doubt about it art can’t be taught and talent can’t be learned. It’s fate that decides who to give that instinct and passion to… but it’s not by chance that you can drink the Espresso Coffee par excellence only through Italian Coffee machines and Tea through Indian Tea machines.

Nothing of what we have experienced during our detailed research into our coffee journey can be compared to the perfection of an Italian cup of coffee or a cup of Black or dark golden tea.

Understanding this we realized that we don’t have to research for years to obtain that impeccable taste.

We started importing Italian/Indian machines INSTEAD to get a taste of a true black masterpiece. Here at Coffee Services, the perfect coffee & Tea is always at hand. So the next time you enjoy a nice cup of coffee think for a moment about the old sailing vessels that crossed the Mediterranean Sea, loaded with coffee way back in the 16th century… and be grateful to Prospero Alpino who brought the first sacks of coffee from the east to Italy… or Thomas Garway, a tobacconist and coffee house owner, brought to England in 1657 from Asia…

For us it’s not just about science or techniques… it’s more about emotions, feelings and nuances that go straight to the heart.

For us at Coffee Services Coffee and Chai has always been a friend, a symbol of the pleasure of getting together, an excuse to catch up and talk… but also to take a break, even alone.
